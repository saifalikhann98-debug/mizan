Is your rent increase legal?

In Dubai Marina, as across Dubai, a renewal increase is capped by Decree 43 of 2013 — nothing if your rent is within 10% of the market average, rising in steps to a maximum of 20% if it sits far below, with 90 days' written notice required. Check your exact case with the calculator.

Common questions

How much is rent for a 1-bedroom in Dubai Marina?

A 1-bedroom apartment in Dubai Marina is typically advertised around AED 88,000 to AED 145,000 per year in 2026. Tenants often sign below the top of that range.

Can my landlord raise my rent in Dubai Marina?

Only within Dubai's RERA cap (Decree 43/2013): no increase if your rent is within 10% of the market average, and 5–20% if it sits further below — with 90 days' written notice.
