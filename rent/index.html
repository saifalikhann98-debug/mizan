How Dubai's rent-increase cap works

Under Dubai Decree (43) of 2013, the maximum your landlord can raise the rent depends on how far your current rent sits below the average market rent (the RERA rental index) for similar properties:

Your rent vs market averageMax increase
Up to 10% below0%
11% – 20% below5%
21% – 30% below10%
31% – 40% below15%
More than 40% below20%

Guidance only — not legal advice. The authoritative market figure comes from the Dubai Land Department's rental index / calculator, and a 90-day written notice is required before any change. Other emirates set their own rules.
