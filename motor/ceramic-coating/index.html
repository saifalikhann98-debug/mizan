Common questions

How much does ceramic coating cost in Dubai?

Ceramic coating typically runs around AED 935 to 2,730 in the UAE — toward the lower end for Japanese/economy cars at an independent garage, higher for German or luxury brands and at dealers.

Why does ceramic coating vary so much between garages?

It depends on your car's make (parts cost), whether parts are genuine or aftermarket, labour rates, and dealer vs independent garage. That's why Mizan shows the typical range next to what drivers actually pay.

Is a dealer or an independent garage cheaper?

For ceramic coating, independent garages are usually cheaper than dealers — often noticeably — but check reviews and ask about parts and any warranty.
