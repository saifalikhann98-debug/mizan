How much does AC cleaning cost in the UAE?

AC cleaning in the UAE is typically advertised between AED 150 and AED 350 per unit, with most quotes around AED 250. Actual prices swing with your area, the provider, and what is included, so the advertised rate is rarely the price you have to pay.
