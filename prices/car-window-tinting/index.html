How much does car window tinting cost in the UAE?

Car window tinting in the UAE is typically advertised between AED 300 and AED 1,500 per car, with most quotes around AED 700. Actual prices swing with your area, the provider, and what is included, so the advertised rate is rarely the price you have to pay.
