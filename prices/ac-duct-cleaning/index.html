How much does AC duct cleaning cost in the UAE?

AC duct cleaning in the UAE is typically advertised between AED 300 and AED 1,200 per job, with most quotes around AED 600. Actual prices swing with your area, the provider, and what is included, so the advertised rate is rarely the price you have to pay.
