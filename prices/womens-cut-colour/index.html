How much does women's cut & colour cost in the UAE?

Women's cut & colour in the UAE is typically advertised between AED 200 and AED 800 per visit, with most quotes around AED 400. Actual prices swing with your area, the provider, and what is included, so the advertised rate is rarely the price you have to pay.
