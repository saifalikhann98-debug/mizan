Common questions

How much should AC cleaning cost in Dubai?

A standard split-unit AC service is usually advertised around AED 150 to 350 in Dubai. Many residents pay less than the top of that range, which is exactly the gap Mizan shows you.

What is a fair price for a men's haircut in the UAE?

Men's haircuts in the UAE commonly run from about AED 40 at budget barbers to AED 150 at premium salons. Mizan compares the advertised rate with what people report actually paying in your area.

How does Mizan work?

Pick a service and your area, optionally enter the price you were quoted, and Mizan instantly shows the advertised market range, the resident-reported paid range, and a clear verdict: fair, steep, or walk away.

Is Mizan free to use?

Yes, Mizan is completely free. It is built on prices reported by the community, never paid placements from businesses.

Which services and areas does Mizan cover?

Mizan covers dozens of everyday services, from AC cleaning, salon and gym to movers, pest control and car service, across all seven UAE emirates.

Where do the prices come from?

Market estimates are advertised list prices from the web; the paid range comes from residents reporting what they actually paid, with outlier trimming so one odd price cannot skew the result.
