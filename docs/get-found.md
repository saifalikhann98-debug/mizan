# Mizan — the "Get Found on Google" checklist

One page. Do it in order. The goal isn't "#1 for fair price" (that term belongs to dictionaries
and the FairPrice supermarket chain) — it's **get indexed, then rank the specific local searches
your customers actually type** ("AC service cost Dubai", "is my rent increase legal", "BMW clutch
cost Dubai", "fair price Dubai"). Those are winnable. This is the sequence to win them.

---

## ✅ Step 2 — Post this week (THIS is the real lever)

Indexing gets you *into* Google. Real visitors + links are what make you *rank*. Pick these 3,
copy from [`community-posts.md`](community-posts.md), and post. **Disclose it's yours** (Reddit/FB
are fine with that if the tool is genuinely useful).

**① Reddit — reply to existing threads (best ROI, do first)**
- Go to r/dubai (also r/UAE, r/dubaicentral). In the subreddit search, look up: `overpaying`,
  `is this fair`, `rent increase`, `garage quote`, `landlord` — sort by **New**.
- Reply to *live* threads with the matching ready-made reply:
  - "landlord raising my rent" → use **R1** (the RERA calculator).
  - "how much is rent in [area]" → use **R2** (link the area guide).
  - "is my garage quote a rip-off" → use **M1**.
  - "how much should a [clutch/AC/service] cost" → use **M2** (link the specific guide).
- Replying to existing threads beats standalone posts — the intent is already there.

**② Facebook groups — one post each**
- Groups: "Dubai Expats", "Dubai Q&A", your area group (Marina/JLT/Al Qusais…), nationality groups.
- Use **R3** (rent) or **M4** (motor) — whichever fits the group.

**③ Your own WhatsApp building / community groups (easiest, warmest)**
- Use **M5** or **R4** — one line + the link. These are people who trust you already.

## ✅ Step 3 — Make ONE bucket "real" (the proof that sells everything)

Your moat is real crowd prices, but the paid layer is still empty. Don't spread thin — pick **one
slice: AC service, Al Qusais** and get it past **5 real submissions** (ask in a relevant thread or
your WhatsApp group). The moment it flips from "estimate" → "what residents actually paid", you have
a live demo that proves the whole product — and every future post becomes far more shareable.

---

## What to expect (so you don't panic)

Ranking is *earned over time*, not switched on. Rough timeline once you've done Steps 1–2:
- **Days:** pages start appearing in Google. Check with `site:mizan-price.com` in Google search.
- **2–6 weeks:** you start ranking for the specific long-tail ("BMW clutch cost Dubai") — *if*
  you've got a trickle of traffic/links from the posting.
- **Months:** broader terms ("fair price Dubai", "car service cost UAE") as the domain builds trust.

If nothing's indexed after a week, the cause is almost always **not enough external signal** — i.e.
do more of Step 2.

## How to check progress (weekly, 2 minutes)
- **Google:** search `site:mizan-price.com` — the number of pages should climb toward ~214.
- **Search Console → Pages:** "Indexed" count going up; "Discovered – not indexed" converting.
- **Search Console → Performance:** impressions, then clicks, start appearing (what you rank for).
- **Vercel Analytics:** real visitors arriving (proof the posting is working).
